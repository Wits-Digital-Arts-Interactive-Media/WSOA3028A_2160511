Welcome to KceeArts! Here We Explore the Digital Realm!

As a multifaceted student and professional in animation, game development, web development, and graphic design, this website showcases my work and expertise. Here, you'll find a diverse portfolio that highlights my creative journey and technical skills across these exciting fields. Whether you're fascinated by the captivating world of animation, intrigued by the complex process of game development, interested in the dynamic realm of web development, or inspired by graphic design, there's something here for everyone.

In addition to my portfolio, this website hosts a series of insightful blog posts. These posts delve into various aspects of animation and web development, providing detailed process overviews and explorations of the industry. As we engage with scholarly readings and current developments, you'll gain a deeper understanding of these disciplines.


Feel free to contact me for any inquiries, whether you have questions about my work, are interested in collaboration opportunities, or simply want to learn more about these fascinating fields. Your engagement and curiosity are highly valued, and I'm here to share my knowledge and passion with you.


Thank you for visiting KceeArts. Let's embark on this creative journey together!

About the Author:

Kaycee Chantelle Mashilo is an animation enthusiast with a passion for storytelling through motion. She is currently studying at University of Witwatersrand towards a Digital Artist degree. She has worked in the digital industry for a one year internship and has chosen to rather focus on cultivating her skills for freelance projects in the industry whilst studying. Working hard everyday to learn new skills and improve is her driving force towards success as a professional, sharing the love for her work and experience along the journey. Her main passions revolve around:

  • Animation
  • Game Development.
  • Web Development
  • Graphics
  • Traditional Visual Art

Creating this Blog series is intended to share more information about her passions as researching is imperative for her growth in knowledge. Learning is important to her and she encourages it for others.
